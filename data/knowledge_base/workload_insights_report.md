# Workload and Learning Correlation Report (Synthetic)

Insights:
- Employees with more than 20 meeting hours per week show lower study completion.
- Optimal completion appears when learners have 12–18 meeting hours and at least 15 focus hours.
- Study reminders should avoid heavy meeting periods and use preferred learning slots when available.

Recommendation:
Schedule learning blocks during focus-heavy periods and keep reminders supportive, not punitive.
